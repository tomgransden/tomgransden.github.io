# End-to-end testing options for React Native

A focussed, solid end-to-end (E2E) test suite can be a life saver for software development teams. By targeting the business critial flows, it can provide early feedback on issues and save manual QA time by checking the routine stuff for you.

There are numerous options available to achieve e2e testing in React Native:

- Maestro
- Detox
- Appium
- Playwright/Appetize
